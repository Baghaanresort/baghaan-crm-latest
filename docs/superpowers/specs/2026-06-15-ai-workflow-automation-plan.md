# Using AI to Help Run the Resort Workflow — Simple Plan

**Date:** 2026-06-15 · **Status:** Just an idea for now (nothing built yet)

**The aim:** let AI help with the whole journey — from a guest's first enquiry, to
booking, to check-in, to checkout — so staff do less typing and chasing.

## The golden rule

AI is a **helper, not the boss.** It reads messages, writes drafts, and keeps an eye on
things. But a staff member always presses the final button on anything to do with
**money, rooms, or messages sent to guests.** This keeps you safe from mistakes.

## How AI helps at each step

| Step | What AI does for you |
|---|---|
| **New enquiry comes in** | Reads the WhatsApp/email/call note and fills the enquiry form automatically. Warns if it's a repeat guest. |
| **Sending a quote** | Writes a ready-to-send price quote and reply (checking which rooms are free). Staff just review and send. |
| **Following up** | Reminds staff about enquiries going cold and suggests the next step. Reads payment screenshots to speed up confirmation. |
| **Before arrival / check-in** | Sends arrival reminders. Reads the guest's ID photo and fills in their details. |
| **During the stay** | A WhatsApp helper takes guest requests and passes them to the right team (kitchen, housekeeping, etc.). |
| **Checkout** | Prepares the final bill, double-checks it, and later asks the guest for a review. |

## A few simple rules to stay safe

- AI can **prepare** things, but a person **approves** anything involving money or rooms.
- AI always uses the real, live data (room availability, rates) — it never guesses.
- Every AI suggestion is recorded, along with who approved it.
- Guest details are sensitive — handle them carefully.
- AI costs money per use, so start small and grow.

## Suggested order to build it

1. **Set up the basics** — connect the AI and test it on one small task.
2. **Start here → Enquiry help + follow-up reminders.** Biggest time-saver, no money involved, very safe.
3. **Quotes + payment-check help.**
4. **Check-in / checkout help** (ID reading, bill check).
5. **Guest WhatsApp helper** (build this last).

**Best first step:** the enquiry + follow-up helper — it saves the most staff effort,
never touches money, and a person checks everything.
